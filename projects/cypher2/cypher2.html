Notes

  • The cipher operates on the Latin alphabet only; digits, punctuation, and whitespace pass through unchanged.
  • Decryption is simply encryption with the key negated — the same machinery, run in reverse.
  • For any key k not coprime with 26, the cipher is its own inverse under a 26-period.
